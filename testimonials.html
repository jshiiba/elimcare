"The decision to move a loved one into a care facility is one of the hardest decisions a family has to make. When the time came to move my husband, we visited several homes, but there was no comparison between Elim Care facilities and the others. The owner came to our home to personally meet my husband, then after meeting him and seeing his home situation, she selected the Elim home that would best fit him. From the day we moved him into Elim Glen, he was immediately part of their family, and I really mean family.

Each of the caregivers treated him with such special care, just like they would their own family member. Whether it was fixing a special meal of his favorite food, helping him exercise his mind and muscles or just providing a special treat, they make it a point to learn about each person and their life experiences. They truly turned a difficult time into a welcoming experience for our family."

- Carolyn (Spouse of former resident)

"My mother was one of the many elderly afflicted by Alzheimer's dementia. When my mother could no longer be adequately supervised by a larger dementia care facility I turned to the more intimate care she could receive at a residential board and care facility. That’s when I found Elim Care.

My mother survived an additional five years benefiting from extremely attentive and individualized care. As her needs changed, I was able to have her transferred from an Elim Care facility in Folsom to a facility in my neighborhood of Woodbridge Ranch in Roseville. This facility has been operating in my neighborhood for more than 10 years and the vast majority of my neighbors do not even know that a dementia care facility is operating in this house.The house blends into our neighborhood that seamlessly.

As a urologist, I deal with the elderly on a daily basis. Our society is in for a shock as the ranks of the physically well elderly with dementia expands. I only wish there were more quality facilities like those of Elim Care."

- Steven (Urologist M.D.)

I have known (the owner) for over 15 years and she is highly-respected by both professionals and families for the love, compassion and quality care she provides to her residents. Elim Care homes by far exceed the standard of care family's come to expect."
