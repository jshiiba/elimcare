Our Mission

Here at Elim Care, we provide personalized care for the elderly afflicted with Alzheimer’s and related dementia. Our goals at Elim Care are designed to encourage and support residents to function at their highest possible level, while providing necessary and personalized assistance and daily activities when needed.


Elim Care seeks to create and maintain an environment that supports socialization and promotes a feeling of meaning and dignity within each of our residents. The various programs offered provide not only the opportunities to fulfill psychosocial needs, but physical support as well.
